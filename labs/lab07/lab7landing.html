Lab 7 - Introduction to Human Computer Interaction: HCI

During this Lab we utilized the methodoligies learned in a guest lecture to understand how users interact with computers and what stimulates the best interactions. We created our group directories for our web-program projects and furthered our website applications by designing the interface, IA, and creating personas to better understand the usability of the project.


Our web-program is designed to help first year RPI students find what classes they have with their friends and what times they all have free to hang out. We plan to make the users manually input their courses or upload an .ics (like from QUACS) and then upload the information there. We have decided to make certain elements like the header and footer PHP and the calendar display possibly using AJAX. The site will be dynamic with jQuery and AJAX and have a simplistic, easy to use design. Below is the link to our group directory where our project and its process is documented. Check it Out!
